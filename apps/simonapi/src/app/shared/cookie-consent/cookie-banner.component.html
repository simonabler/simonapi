Cookies & external services

SimonAPI uses only one technically necessary cookie to store your choice. Your decision is saved in the cookie simonapi-consent (retention: 180 days, SameSite=Lax).

  • Necessary: simonapi-consent records whether you have allowed necessary and/or optional cookies.
  • Functional: The web tools operate statelessly – API calls do not set any browser cookies.
  • External media: The avatar image on the home page is loaded directly from gravatar.com; your IP address is transmitted to Automattic in the process.
  • No analytics/marketing: No tracking, advertising or social-media scripts are active.
simonapi-consent
Purpose: Stores your current cookie settings. Category: technically necessary. Retention: 180 days. Content: flags for necessary/optional and a timestamp.
Gravatar
When you visit the home page, the avatar is loaded from https://www.gravatar.com/. The service sets no cookies for image delivery but does process your IP address.
API calls
All tools send requests directly to SimonAPI. No session or tracking cookies are currently returned.
